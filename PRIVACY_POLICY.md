# Economic Influence Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 18, 2024**

## Introduction

Economic Influence is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

## Key Principles

### 1. All Data Stored Locally
All data you enter into Economic Influence is stored **exclusively on your device**. We do not operate any servers, cloud infrastructure, or backend services that receive your data.

### 2. No Data Collection
We do **not** collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information or purchase data. Your purchase history, spending patterns, and any other information you enter never leaves your device.

### 3. No Account Required
Economic Influence does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal information to use the app.

### 4. No Tracking or Analytics
We do **not** use:
- Analytics services
- Tracking technologies
- Advertising networks
- Third-party data collection tools

## Camera Usage

The camera feature is used **only** for receipt scanning. When you use this feature:
- Images are processed entirely on your device
- No images are transmitted to any server
- No images are stored permanently unless you explicitly save them
- OCR (text recognition) happens on-device

## Data Storage

### What We Store
- Your purchase entries (store name, amount, date, notes)
- These are stored in your device's local storage using SharedPreferences

### What We Don't Store
- Personal identifiers
- Location data
- Contact information
- Any data on external servers

## Third-Party Services

Economic Influence does not integrate with any third-party services that could access your data.

## Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:
- Your data is as secure as your device
- We recommend using device-level security (PIN, fingerprint, face recognition)
- Uninstalling the app will delete all associated data

## Children's Privacy

Economic Influence is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store listing.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data:
- There is no data to access, correct, or delete on our servers
- You can clear all app data through the Settings screen in the app
- You can delete the app to remove all data from your device

## Compliance

This app complies with:
- Apple App Store privacy requirements
- Google Play Store privacy requirements
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles (we don't process personal data)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) (we don't sell personal information)

---

**Summary: Economic Influence is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your data stays on your device, period.**
